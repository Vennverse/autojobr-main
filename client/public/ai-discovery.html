What is AutoJobR?

AutoJobR is a free, AI-powered job application automation platform that helps job seekers apply to 1000+ jobs daily, beat Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS), and get hired 10x faster. The platform uses advanced artificial intelligence to match candidates with relevant jobs, optimize resumes, and automate the entire application process.

  • Automated Job Applications: Apply to 1000+ jobs daily automatically across LinkedIn, Indeed, and 100+ job boards
  • ATS Resume Optimization: AI analyzes and optimizes resumes to pass Applicant Tracking Systems
  • LinkedIn Auto-Apply: One-click applications directly on LinkedIn with browser extension
