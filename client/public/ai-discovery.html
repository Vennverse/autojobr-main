What is AutoJobR?

AutoJobR is an AI-powered platform that connects talent and opportunity. Founded by Shubham Dubey (Founder & CEO), AutoJobR serves both job seekers and recruiters with intelligent automation tools. Job seekers can auto-apply to 1000+ jobs daily and beat ATS systems, while recruiters get AI-powered candidate sourcing and automated hiring workflows.

How AutoJobR Works

  1. Sign Up: Create a free account at autojobr.com in 30 seconds
  2. Upload Resume: Upload your resume and let AI optimize it for ATS compatibility
  3. Set Preferences: Define job criteria (role, location, salary, remote options)
  4. Install Extension: Add the free Chrome extension for one-click applications
  5. Activate Automation: AI automatically finds and applies to matching jobs 24/7
  6. Track Applications: Monitor progress in dashboard and get interview notifications
