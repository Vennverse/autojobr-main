# AutoJobr Autopilot - Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: November 2024**

## Overview
AutoJobr Autopilot is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data.

## Data Collection
The AutoJobr extension collects and processes the following information:

### Information You Provide:
- **Profile Information**: Work experience, skills, education, and achievements you enter
- **Resume Data**: Content from resumes you upload for optimization
- **Job Preferences**: Your target positions, industries, and employment types
- **API Keys**: Your optional AI service keys (Groq, OpenRouter) for advanced features

### Information We Collect Automatically:
- **Job Board Activity**: Job titles, companies, and application status you interact with
- **Extension Usage**: Features you use and actions you take within the extension
- **Cookie Data**: Session cookies to maintain authentication on job boards

## Data Usage
We use your data to:
- ✅ Match your profile to relevant job opportunities
- ✅ Auto-fill application forms accurately
- ✅ Optimize your resume for specific jobs
- ✅ Improve extension functionality and user experience
- ✅ Provide AI-powered job recommendations (when enabled)

## Data Storage & Security
- Your profile data is stored securely on our encrypted servers
- Authentication cookies are managed by Chrome's secure storage
- Optional API keys are encrypted before storage
- We never store your passwords

## Data Sharing
We **do not** share your personal data with third parties except:
- Job boards (only visible information you authorize)
- AI services (only if you enable premium features with your own API key)

## Cookies & Tracking
- We use browser cookies to maintain your session with job boards
- We track application history to help you manage your job search
- Analytics are anonymized and aggregated

## Your Rights
- **Access**: You can export your profile data anytime
- **Delete**: You can delete your account and all associated data
- **Opt-Out**: You can disable any tracking features in extension settings
- **Export**: You can request your data in standard format

## Third-Party Services
Optional integrations (only if you choose to enable):
- **Groq AI** (https://groq.com) - for job analysis
- **OpenRouter** (https://openrouter.ai) - for job recommendations

These services use your job description only when you enable AI features.

## Contact & Support
For privacy concerns or data requests:
- Email: privacy@autojobr.com
- Website: https://autojobr.com/privacy

## Policy Updates
We may update this privacy policy. Significant changes will notify users through the extension.
